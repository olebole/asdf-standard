parabolic: Parabolic projection.¶

Type: pseudocylindrical-1.0.0.

Parabolic projection.

Corresponds to the PAR projection in the FITS WCS standard.

The pixel-to-sky transformation is defined as:

\[\begin{split}\phi &= \frac{180^\circ}{\pi} \frac{x}{1 - 4(y / 180^\circ)^2} \\ \theta &= 3 \sin^{-1}\left(\frac{y}{180^\circ}\right)\end{split}\]

And the sky-to-pixel transformation is defined as:

\[\begin{split}x &= \phi \left(2\cos\frac{2\theta}{3} - 1\right) \\ y &= 180^\circ \sin \frac{\theta}{3}\end{split}\]

Invertibility: All ASDF tools are required to provide the inverse of this transform.
